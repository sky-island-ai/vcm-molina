Introducing the Virtual Case Manager

A dedicated, AI-powered assistant for every member, deployed under your brand to provide proactive, personalized, and unlimited support, 24/7.

Augmentation, Not Replacement

Our core philosophy is to give time back to Case Managers, empowering them to focus on what humans do best: complex care and clinical intervention.

Initial HRA outreach, appointment reminders, deadline notifications, and basic, repetitive member questions.

What Human CMs Focus On
